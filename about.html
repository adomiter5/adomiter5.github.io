About this Anthology

My name is Andrea, and I am the creator of this website. As the culminating assignment for the Poetry in the Land of Childhood course at the University of Chicago, I was tasked with preparing an anthology of my choice. My anthology is on the role of animals in children's poems. I decided to showcase my anthology via this website. The creation of this website would not have been possible if I had not taken Data Publication for the Humanities, another course offered at the University of Chicago.

It should be noted that all photos and videos used in this website belong to their rightful owners. No credit is taken. This website is for personal use.
